Clean Energy Comsuption Estimates by State 2015

The map is presentinng the Clean Energy Comsuption in US.The data is taken form State Energy Data System.Due to the limitation that all the environment, the expendtion of the clean energy is restrictied in certain states.

  • Hydro: the annual production of coal in 2015 by State. The data taken for produciton is marketed production, and the refuse recovery is inculded. The unit for the data presented for coal produciton is Thousand Short Tons.
  • Nuclear: the annual production of natural gas in 2015 by State. Includes offshore production of natural gas in the Gulf of Mexico and the refuse recovery is inculded. The unit for the data presented for natural gas produciton is Million Cubic Feet.
  • Wood and Waste: the annual production of crude oil in 2015 by State. Inculded the offshore production of crude oil in the Gulf of Mexico and along the Pacific coast. The unit for the data presented for oil produciton is Thousand Barrels.
  • Solar: the annual production of renewable Ethanol in 2015 by State. The data taken for produciton is marketed production, and the refuse recovery is inculded. The unit for the data presented for Ethanol produciton is Thousand Barrels.

Overall, there is a clear difference on the energy type. The main produciton of coal is concentrated arround the central US arround the mountain area. The Oil and Natural Gas are concentrated on the west and southern cost. The renewable Ethanol fuel is less popular in urban and deserted area, but brooms in agricultural states.
